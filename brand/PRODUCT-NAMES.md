# JALVORO product family

- JALVORO — master platform
- Jalvoro Personal — personal money and life workspace
- Jalvoro Business — small-business workspace
- Jalvoro POS — point-of-sale experience
- Jalvoro ERP — advanced company operations
- Jalvoro CRM — customer and sales relationships

Product labels remain configurable in `brand.config.ts`; technical module routes and database identifiers do not need to match public names.
